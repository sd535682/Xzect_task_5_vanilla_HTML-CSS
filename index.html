Frequently Asked Questions

Let's take a moment to address some frequently asked questions (FAQs) that users commonly inquire about with us regarding our adoption process and pet care information.

Q. How can I adopt a dog from your store?

To adopt a dog from our store, start by visiting us during our adoption hours. Our friendly staff will guide you through the process of meeting our available dogs and help you find a perfect match. You'll need to fill out an adoption application, meet with the dog you're interested in, and if it's a good fit, finalize the adoption with an adoption fee.

Q. What is included in the adoption process?

Our adoption process includes a comprehensive health check-up, vaccinations up to date, and microchipping for identification. Each adopted dog also comes with a starter pack of food, basic supplies, and valuable information on care and training. Our goal is to ensure each dog finds a loving and responsible forever home.

Q. Can I adopt if I live outside area?

Yes, we welcome adopters from outside our immediate area. However, please note that some dogs may require a home check or additional steps to ensure their well-being and transition. We strive to facilitate adoptions wherever possible, ensuring each dog finds a suitable home where they will be loved and cared for.
